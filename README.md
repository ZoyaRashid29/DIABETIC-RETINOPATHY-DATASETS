# RetinoXAI – Data Sources

This repository provides information about the datasets used in the research work titled:

**"RetinoXAI: Diagnosis of Diabetic Retinopathy using Federated Learning and Explainable AI"**

Due to the large size of the datasets and licensing restrictions, the datasets are not hosted directly in this repository. Instead, publicly available third party datasets were used, and their official access links are provided below.

---

## Datasets Used

### 1. Diabetic Retinopathy Balanced Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- Description: Retinal fundus images labeled according to the severity of diabetic retinopathy (five-class classification).
- Link:  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kushagratandon12/diabetic-retinopathy-balanced

---

### 2. Diabetic Retinopathy 224×224 Gaussian Filtered Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- Description: Preprocessed retinal images resized to 224×224 resolution with Gaussian filtering applied.
- Link:  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sovitrath/diabetic-retinopathy-224x224-gaussian-filtered

---

## Data Usage & Licensing

All datasets are publicly available and were used strictly for research and academic purposes.  
The original dataset licenses and terms of use apply as specified by their respective providers on Kaggle.

No new patient data were collected, and no personally identifiable information is included in this study.
